# Naming Decision - Yazata

## Objective

Define a devotional brand name that honors God and still communicates the tracker purpose of the application.

## 1) Semantic Axes

- Devotional axis: worship, praise, remembrance of God, watchfulness in prayer.
- Tracker axis: register, track, follow progress, keep a daily log.

## 2) Root Bank (validated meanings)

### Greek (Koine/Ancient)

- `doxa` (doxa): glory, honor, praise.
- `latreia` (latreia): worship, sacred service to God.
- `anamnesis` (anamnesis): active remembrance, memorial.
- `mnemosynon` (mnemosynon): memorial, remembrance.

### Classical Arabic

- `dhikr` (dhikr): remembrance (commonly remembrance of God).
- `hamd` (hamd): praise, exaltation, reverent gratitude.
- `muraqabah` (muraqabah): spiritual watchfulness, awareness that God observes.

### Avestan / Old Persian tradition

- `yaz` (yaz): to worship, to venerate.
- `yazata` (yazata): worthy of worship or veneration.
- `yasna` (yasna): worship, liturgical prayer.
- `mazda` (mazda): wisdom (notably in Ahura Mazda).

## 5) Viability Checks (quick pass)

- Pronunciation:
  - `Yazata` can be spoken in PT-BR as "ia-ZA-ta" (approximation).
  - International references often render it close to "yah-ZAH-tuh".
- Domain/trademark:
  - Web search did not provide conclusive WHOIS results for `yazata.com` or `yazata.app`.
  - There are related names in software/consulting space (for example, Yazata Consulting), so legal due diligence is still required before final registration.
- Recommendation:
  - Keep `Yazata` as primary brand and test domain alternatives (`getyazata.com`, `yazataapp.com`, `yazatatracker.com`) if the exact domain is unavailable.

## 6) Final Scoring and Decision

### Scoring scale

1 to 5 on: devotional fidelity, tracker clarity, memorability, originality, availability signal.

### Top 3 Result

1. `Yazata` (Primary)
   - Strong devotional identity ("worthy of worship/veneration"), short and memorable.
2. `YazataTrack` (Backup 1)
   - Keeps devotional core and makes product function explicit.
3. `YazataLog` (Backup 2)
   - Strong fit for daily records and spiritual discipline workflows.

## Brand Direction Chosen

- Primary product name: `Yazata`
- Suggested descriptor: `Yazata - Faith Tracker`
